Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the 21-Day High Ticket Sales Challenge

What makes this challenge different from other real estate training programs?

Unlike generic real estate courses, our challenge focuses specifically on high-ticket property sales and the unique strategies required to succeed in this lucrative market. We bring together six industry experts with specialized knowledge in different aspects of premium real estate sales, from property evaluation to AI-powered marketing to luxury client psychology.

Additionally, our interactive format with daily Q&A sessions ensures you get personalized guidance for your specific situation, not just theoretical knowledge.

I'm new to real estate. Is this challenge suitable for beginners?

While experienced agents will certainly benefit, this challenge is specifically designed to help newer agents bypass years of trial and error by learning directly from those who have already mastered high-ticket sales.

In fact, many new agents find it easier to transition directly to premium properties rather than trying to "work their way up" through lower-priced listings. We'll provide you with the mindset, strategies, and scripts you need regardless of your experience level.

How is the challenge structured? What's the time commitment?

The challenge runs for 21 consecutive days from May 18th to June 7th, 2025. Each day includes:

  • A 45-minute expert training session
  • A 30-minute Q&A period
  • A daily implementation assignment (15-30 minutes)

All sessions are recorded, so if you miss a live session, you can catch up at your convenience. The total time commitment is approximately 1.5-2 hours per day.

Will I have access to the materials after the challenge ends?

Yes! You'll receive lifetime access to all training recordings, resources, templates, and scripts. This allows you to revisit the material whenever you need a refresher or want to implement a specific strategy.

Additionally, you'll maintain access to our private community where you can continue to network with fellow participants and share successes and challenges.

What results can I expect from this challenge?

While results vary based on your market, experience, and implementation, past participants have reported:

  • Closing their first high-ticket property within 90 days of completing the challenge
  • Increasing their average commission by 200-400%
  • Building a network of high-net-worth clients and referral sources
  • Developing the confidence to pursue premium listings they previously thought were out of reach

The challenge provides you with all the tools, strategies, and support you need to succeed in high-ticket real estate sales. Your results will depend on how consistently you apply what you learn.

Is there a guarantee or refund policy?

Yes, we offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee. If you participate in the first week of the challenge (attend or watch the recordings and complete the assignments) and don't feel it's providing value, you can request a full refund.

Simply email our support team with your completed assignments and feedback on why the program didn't meet your expectations, and we'll process your refund promptly.

How many participants will be in the challenge?

To ensure personalized attention and high-quality interaction, we limit each challenge to 100 participants. This allows our experts to address individual questions during the Q&A sessions and provide meaningful feedback.

Previous challenges have sold out quickly, so we recommend securing your spot early to avoid disappointment.
